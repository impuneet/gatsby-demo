Hey, I'm Radhika Morabia. Welcome to my personal website. I'm really happy to have you here, and I hope you stick around and explore a bit.

Radhika who?

Radhika Morabia. That's me to the right. I'm a 17-year-old from the suburbs of Los Angeles, California and I'm obsessed with achieving success.

Just a few years ago, I was a top student aiming to go to one of the top 30 universities in the US. Then, in my Freshman year of high school, I injured myself by following the typical high school advice to do everything. After months of seeing doctors and failing to find a solution, I eventually had to leave my higih school.

I was dragged through alternative school after alternative school all while struggling to handle the constant pain my injury gave me. I eventually dropped out of school entirely. Since then, I've been working on creating opportunities for myself.

I began networking with people I admire, I started a consulting business, and I've been reading a lot of books. This blog exists as the culmination of everything I've learned as I continue along this journey. I'm no expert, but everything I write about has been tried and tested by me for months before publishing.
