Thoughts:

The track begins pretty mediocre, and this is a constant in some Frank Turner songs. If you don't give it time, you won't reach the best part, which is around the last minute. It's almost like it escalates, which is kind of odd in a 4-minute esong. I don't think most listeners would have the patience for that, and judgements are usually made so quickly.

The last minute is glorious, and so worth the waiting from the first two minutes. The strings come in and the whole song just feels fuller. Then, you hear the album title, and you know it's serious now.

I find this track to be hopeful. The story behind the song is after Frank went to China and did successful shows there, he realized that rock is everywhere and beyond that, rock is adored everywhere. It's a pleasant listen with a very happy and upbeat melody.
